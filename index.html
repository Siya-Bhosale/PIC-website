About PIC

Fostering Innovation and Research Excellence at PCCOER

The Project & Innovation Club (PIC) at Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering and Research is dedicated to transforming creative ideas into impactful solutions. We bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, fostering a culture of innovation and research excellence among students.

Our mission is to cultivate a research-driven mindset, encourage innovative thinking, and provide students with the tools and guidance needed to excel in their academic and professional endeavors.

Innovation Focus

Encouraging creative problem-solving and out-of-the-box thinking in technology projects.

Industry Relevance

Connecting academic learning with real-world industry challenges and opportunities.

Community Building

Creating a collaborative environment where students can learn, share, and grow together.

Excellence Recognition

Celebrating and acknowledging outstanding achievements in research and innovation.

Our Mission

To cultivate a vibrant ecosystem of innovation where students transform ideas into impactful solutions, develop cutting-edge projects, and contribute meaningfully to society through research and technological advancement.

What We Offer

Comprehensive programs designed to nurture innovation and research excellence

Research Orientation

Comprehensive introduction to research methodologies, academic writing, and publication strategies.

Innovation Workshops

Hands-on sessions covering design thinking, ideation techniques, and prototype development.

Technical Training

Skill development in cutting-edge technologies, programming languages, and development tools.

Team Collaboration

Multi-disciplinary project teams working together on real-world challenges and solutions.

Patent Guidance

Step-by-step assistance with intellectual property protection and patent filing processes.

Competitions

Participation in national and international competitions, hackathons, and innovation challenges.
